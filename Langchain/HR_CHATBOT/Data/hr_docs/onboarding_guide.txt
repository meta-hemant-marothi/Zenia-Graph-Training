Onboarding Guide - TechNova Solutions

1. Day 1:
   - Welcome session with HR at 10 AM.
   - IT will set up your laptop and access.

2. Week 1:
   - Complete all onboarding modules via the LMS.
   - Attend team intro and shadow a senior teammate.

3. Tools Access:
   - Email, GitHub, JIRA, Confluence, and Zoom setup provided on Day 1.

4. Buddy System:
   - Each new hire is assigned a buddy for the first 30 days to ease transition.

5. First Review:
   - Your manager will schedule a check-in at the end of Week 2 to review progress and goals.
