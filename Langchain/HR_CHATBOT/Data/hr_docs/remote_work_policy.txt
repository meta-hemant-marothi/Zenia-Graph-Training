Remote Work Policy - TechNova Solutions

1. Eligibility:
   - Employees with over 6 months tenure are eligible to apply.
   - Must have no performance issues in the past 2 review cycles.

2. Duration:
   - Up to 3 days per week can be remote.
   - Full-time remote work is allowed for certain roles.

3. Work Hours:
   - Employees must be online during core hours: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM IST.

4. Communication:
   - Teams must use Slack and Zoom for daily stand-ups and meetings.

5. Equipment:
   - The company provides laptops and reimburses up to ₹15,000 for ergonomic setup once every 3 years.
