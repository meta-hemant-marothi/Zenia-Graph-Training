Leave Policy - TechNova Solutions

1. Annual Leave: 18 paid leaves per year.
2. Sick Leave: 12 paid sick leaves annually.
3. Casual Leave: 8 casual leaves per calendar year.
4. Maternity Leave: Up to 26 weeks as per government norms.
5. Paternity Leave: 10 days of paid leave.
6. Leave Approval: Leaves must be applied via the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager.
7. Carry Forward: A maximum of 10 unused annual leaves can be carried forward to the next year.
