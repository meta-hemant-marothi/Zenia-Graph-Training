Reimbursement Policy - TechNova Solutions

1. Internet & Phone:
   - Monthly allowance of ₹1,000 for employees working remotely.

2. Travel:
   - Domestic travel must be pre-approved by the manager.
   - Flight, taxi, and hotel bills must be submitted within 7 days.

3. Learning:
   - Courses up to ₹15,000/year can be reimbursed upon manager approval.

4. Meal Coupons:
   - All full-time employees receive digital food coupons worth ₹2,000/month.

5. Submission Process:
   - Reimbursements must be submitted via the HRMS portal with original bills attached.
